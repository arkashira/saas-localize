## Customer Segments
* SaaS engineering teams with international customer bases
* Companies with existing localization workflows seeking automation
* Small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs) with limited translation resources
* Enterprises with complex translation requirements
* Primary target market: US and European SaaS companies with expansion plans in Asia

* Automated translation pipeline reducing manual bottlenecks by up to 80%
* Integration with popular development tools for seamless localization
* Support for multiple languages, including Thai, Chinese, and Japanese
* Real-time collaboration and project management features
* Cost savings: up to $10,000 per month (approximately 300,000 THB) for large enterprises

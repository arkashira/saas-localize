# Customer Journey Map
## Aware Phase
### Trigger Event
* Receive a notification from a colleague about a new feature or update that requires localization
* Attend a conference or workshop on SaaS engineering and hear about the importance of localization
* Search online for solutions to manual translation bottlenecks

### Friction Points
* Lack of knowledge about existing localization tools and processes
* Difficulty in finding reliable and accurate translation services
* Concerns about data security and intellectual property protection

### User Emotions
* Frustration with manual translation processes
* Anxiety about meeting deadlines and delivering high-quality localized content
* Curiosity about new technologies and solutions

### Opportunities to Delight
* Provide educational resources and webinars on localization best practices
* Offer free trials or demos of automated translation pipelines
* Highlight success stories and case studies of companies that have implemented localization solutions

### Metric per Phase
* Awareness: 10% of target audience is aware of the pain point and potential solutions (metric: social media engagement, blog post views)
* Consider: 20% of aware individuals consider implementing a localization solution (metric: free trial sign-ups, demo requests)

## Consider Phase
### Trigger Event
* Receive a quote from a translation agency or freelancer
* Compare prices and features of different localization tools and services
* Evaluate the return on investment (ROI) of implementing a localization solution

### Friction Points
* High costs associated with manual translation processes
* Difficulty in evaluating the quality and accuracy of translation services
* Concerns about scalability and flexibility of localization solutions

### User Emotions
* Concerned about the financial impact of manual translation processes
* Frustrated with the lack of transparency and control over the translation process
* Excited about the potential benefits of automation and efficiency gains

### Opportunities to Delight
* Offer personalized demos and trials to showcase the benefits of automated translation pipelines
* Provide detailed case studies and ROI analyses of companies that have implemented localization solutions
* Highlight the scalability and flexibility of the solution

### Metric per Phase
* Consider: 50% of individuals who considered implementing a localization solution decide to move forward (metric: free trial conversions, paid subscription sign-ups)
* Try: 80% of individuals who try the solution report a positive experience (metric: user satisfaction surveys, Net Promoter Score)

## Try Phase
### Trigger Event
* Start using the automated translation pipeline for a small project or pilot
* Receive training and support from the Axentx team
* Evaluate the quality and accuracy of the translated content

### Friction Points
* Difficulty in integrating the solution with existing tools and workflows
* Concerns about data security and intellectual property protection
* Frustration with the learning curve and onboarding process

### User Emotions
* Excited about the potential benefits of automation and efficiency gains
* Confident in the quality and accuracy of the translated content
* Frustrated with the complexity and steep learning curve

### Opportunities to Delight
* Offer comprehensive training and support programs
* Provide dedicated account management and customer success teams
* Highlight the scalability and flexibility of the solution

### Metric per Phase
* Try: 90% of individuals who try the solution report a positive experience (metric: user satisfaction surveys, Net Promoter Score)
* Adopt: 80% of individuals who try the solution decide to adopt it as a standard process (metric: paid subscription conversions, customer retention rate)

## Adopt Phase
### Trigger Event
* Receive feedback and testimonials from satisfied customers
* Attend a conference or workshop on SaaS engineering and hear about the success of Axentx's localization solution
* Evaluate the return on investment (ROI) of implementing the solution

### Friction Points
* Concerns about the long-term costs and maintenance of the solution
* Difficulty in evaluating the quality and accuracy of the translated content
* Frustration with the lack of transparency and control over the translation process

### User Emotions
* Confident in the quality and accuracy of the translated content
* Excited about the potential benefits of automation and efficiency gains
* Concerned about the long-term costs and maintenance of the solution

### Opportunities to Delight
* Offer ongoing training and support programs
* Provide dedicated account management and customer success teams
* Highlight the scalability and flexibility of the solution

### Metric per Phase
* Adopt: 90% of individuals who adopt the solution report a positive experience (metric: user satisfaction surveys, Net Promoter Score)
* Expand: 80% of individuals who adopt the solution decide to expand its use to other areas of their business (metric: upsell and cross-sell conversions, customer retention rate)
